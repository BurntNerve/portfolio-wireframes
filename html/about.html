About Me

I’m a full stack web developer from Haymarket, Virginia and a recent graduate of Thinkful’s Full Stack Flex bootcamp. Web development is the perfect synthesis of the what I love to do. It requires an insatiable appetite for learning, a thorough understanding of your role and how it functions within a team, and an intrinsic empathy for people who will interact with what you make. I come from a background in the theatre, so I’ve spent time trying to understand others. Programming possesses that same motivating factor, for me. I’m looking to create things that inspire users to engage with them.
