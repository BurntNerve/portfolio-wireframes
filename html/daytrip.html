Daytripper

Daytripper is a web-app that allows users to select from among several choices and generates agendas of varying sizes, activities, and price ranges. Planning where to go and what to do is often exhausting, however, DayTripper is simplifying the conversation. It streamlines the decision making process by providing users with 3 essential questions: What do you want to do? Where do you want to do it? How much time do you want to spend? How much money do you want to spend?
